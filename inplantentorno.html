inplant is a brand that shows the quality of the air we breathe. Its aim is to raise awareness about the poor quality of the air that surrounds us, especially indoors, and offers a renting of air purifying plants. This brand has been created for the final master's project, carried out with Jonathan Rodríguez, during the Master in Graphic Design 19/20 by ELISAVA. The aim of the project is to create and design the identity of a brand that offers a solution to improve people's quality of life.
